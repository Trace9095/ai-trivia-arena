# AI Trivia Arena — Instagram Marketing Playbook
## 30-Day Calendar: March 21 – April 19, 2026

---

## Brand Overview

**Product:** AI Trivia Arena — fully automated trivia for bars & restaurants, running on their existing TV screens. No host. No setup. Just subscribe, mount the screen, and let the AI run the show.

**How it works:**
- Bar subscribes to AI Trivia Arena
- TV screens show the live game interface
- Players join on their phones — no app download required
- AI rotates categories: pop culture, sports, local knowledge, tech, food & drink
- Leaderboards update in real time, prizes optional, energy is always high

**Two audiences. One product. Different messages:**
- **B2C (Players/Bar-Goers):** "Your bar just got way more fun"
- **B2B (Bar & Restaurant Owners):** "Trivia night = Tuesday revenue you didn't have last month"

**Handle:** @aitriviarena
**Tone:** Game show excitement meets bar-night FOMO. Smart B2B efficiency pitch. Never boring. Always competitive.

---

## 2026 Instagram Algorithm Notes

- **Sends = #1 reach signal.** Every caption should beg to be forwarded. "Send this to your bar" CTAs outperform everything.
- **Hook in frame 1 must work on mute.** Bold text overlays, not voiceover-dependent openings.
- **3–5 hashtags only.** Quality over quantity. Niche + broad mix.
- **Educational content (owner-facing) gets saved.** Saves signal "I'll come back to this" — ideal for B2B posts.
- **Polls and question stickers drive replies.** Replies boost distribution more than likes.
- **Reels under 30 seconds outperform on reach.** Keep 15–22 second range for top-of-funnel.
