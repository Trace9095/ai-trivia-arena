## Full Description

AI Trivia Arena is the multiplayer trivia game that never runs out of questions — because it generates them on the fly.

Traditional trivia games have a fixed question pool. Play long enough and you see the same questions again. AI Trivia Arena solves this completely: its AI engine generates fresh, verified questions across hundreds of topics so every game is genuinely different. You can play every night for a year and never hit a repeat.

Game modes:
- Solo Challenge — race against the clock to see how many you can answer correctly
- Multiplayer Duel — head-to-head live battles against another player in real time
- Group Arena — up to 8 players in a room, competing simultaneously for the top score
- TV Mode — put it on the big screen for parties and bar events; everyone plays on their phones
- Daily Blitz — 10 questions, one shot, global leaderboard. Score resets every 24 hours

AI-powered categories:
- General Knowledge
- Science & Technology
- History & Geography
- Pop Culture & Entertainment
- Sports & Athletes
- Music & Artists
- Food & Drink
- Literature & Books
- Current Events (updated daily)
- Custom categories — ask the AI to generate a round on any topic you choose

What makes it different:
Questions are difficulty-rated in real time based on how the player community answers them. Easy questions that everyone gets right get harder. Tricky questions that stump the room get explained and refined. The AI learns what's too easy and what's too hard, making every session feel perfectly challenging.

Power-ups:
- 50/50 — eliminate two wrong answers
- Skip — replace a question you've never heard of
- Double Points — bet your round on a category you know
- Time Freeze — pause the clock for 10 seconds

Leaderboards:
- Global all-time
- Weekly reset boards
- Friend leaderboards
- Category-specific rankings (are you actually the best at 80s music trivia? Prove it)

TV Mode for events:
Run AI Trivia Arena on a venue TV or projector while players join on their phones. Questions display on the big screen, everyone answers simultaneously, and the live scoreboard shows the battle in real time. Perfect for bar trivia nights, company events, and parties.

For trivia hosts:
Create a private room, choose your categories, set difficulty, and run a curated trivia night without a trivia book or printed sheets. Host controls let you skip questions, pause rounds, and reveal answers dramatically.
